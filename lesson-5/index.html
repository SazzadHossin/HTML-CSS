Mythical Creatures

dragon
A dragon is a magical legendary creature that appears in the folklore of multiple cultures worldwide. Beliefs about dragons vary considerably through regions, but dragons in Western cultures since the High Middle Ages have often been depicted as winged, horned, and capable of breathing fire.
phoenix
"Phoenix" can refer to a mythological immortal bird symbolizing rebirth, the capital city of Arizona, the actor Joaquin Phoenix, or various businesses and programs like the Phoenix Framework or the Phoenix Contact company.
vampire
A vampire is a mythical creature, typically a reanimated corpse, that sustains itself by consuming the vital essence or blood of the living. While varying across different cultures and stories, common characteristics include an undead state, pale skin, fangs, and an insatiable thirst for blood, with victims sometimes becoming vampires themselves.
werewolf
A werewolf is a creature from folklore and legend that is a human capable of transforming into a wolf or a wolf-human hybrid.
